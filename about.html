About Us

Founded in 2020, CleverClash was created to evolve security in this innovative society. We know that losing your wallet can be more than an inconvenience.

In a fast-moving life comes risks of one’s wallet, filled with one’s personal information, being lost, misplaced, or even stolen. That’s why our wallets are designed with built-in, lightweight GPS technology that works with your smartphone to help you locate the moment it's missing. We strive to make your life easier with no inconvenience.
