Instructions on Navigation

1. Download and connect the CleverCash app available on iOS, Android, and other devices

2. Set your own maximum "wallet range" that will alert you if it is out of that specific range.

3. Customize it any way you like!

There are two tracking modes - "off" and "on." While on, the app will alert you when your wallet exceeds its set range. When off, there will not be an alert at all

You can customize the ways in which it alerts you. You can choose to be alerted by any combination of ringing, vibrating, or popup notifications
